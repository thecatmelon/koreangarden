## Why I am learning Korean
**TL;DR: I'm too lazy to read subtitles and thought "learning a whole ass new language will make this easier".**

**Long answer:**
K-pop is one of my main genres, and there's so much content made by the groups I like it's hard to find time to sit down and watch them because I have to read subtitles. I'd love to be able to put on a video while cooking or cleaning, or even to double screen. It would also be nice to watch lives and actually know what the idols are saying.

Also one of my favourite authors is Han Kang, and I lament the fact most of her novels haven't been translated to English, so maybe if I get good enough at this language I can read her work in the original Korean.

- preferably B2, but that's gonna be hard

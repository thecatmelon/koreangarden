[KIWI-Korean Input With Images](https://www.youtube.com/@kiwi-koreaninputwithimages)’s [101 playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLCM7cHg1cZRFLC2reW6e_NHDZLsELFxgw) & [basics](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=j_tWtK6WBBE&list=PLCM7cHg1cZRGyZrBea1-HWWiOHb0qSShn&pp=iAQB): 4 hours; love this channel, incredibly cute & useful. 
>  4. [C.K.W.M](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCxu-uogpSFcy72fz1p_FhpA). / [Min](https://t.umblr.com/redirect?z=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.tiktok.com%2F%40c.k.w.m&t=NWExM2YxNDM4YzQ2NDgyNzgxNjkzMWFlMzFhNWFhNzliNjhhOTU1YSxDdXJpRnBlUA%3D%3D&b=t%3Acassbp1_hRB8tLleMx6YIQ&p=https%3A%2F%2Fnowtoboldlygo.tumblr.com%2Fpost%2F725405588080705536%2Fko-sb-comprehensible-input&m=1&ts=1736268437): shorts/tiktoks 
>  5. [Breeze Korean](https://www.youtube.com/@BreezeKorean): 4-5 hours, super high quality.
 > 6. [Pronounce Korean](https://www.youtube.com/@Pronounce-Korean): 40+ hours; great channel for beginners! clear, repetitive, prolific.
 > 7. [몰입한국어 Immersion in Korean](https://www.youtube.com/@morip.korean)’s [Super Beginner/A0-A1 short story playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLr7BTDhDckaK8V4wBf-EWRtZY94dlAnwp): 5 hours; short stories repeated thrice.
 > 8. [Learn Korean with Dadano](https://www.youtube.com/@korean.with.dadano/)'s beginner videos: 1-2 hours; not sorted into a playlist

[Master Vocabulary Korean](https://www.youtube.com/@mavoca-kor)’s [vocabulary](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLfpiaAH3tVB6G4AUaFhbKEiBhpZFjqr1D) & [verbs](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLfpiaAH3tVB5pteMMmk6GH4uKzt0gEvAX) playlists: 5+ hours; repetitively describes pictures in short videos.
> 10. [Comprehensible Korean](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCxwSrFHHC3I9xFxrChfuqwA/featured): 3-4 hours; more useful to me after the above, but overall good quality!
> 11. [Storytime in Korean](https://www.youtube.com/@storytimeinkorean)’s [A Little to the Left (Beginner Korean)](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLNvUjJNHW4_thdxJu1ZCDIQa30VXojh-V): 1-2 hours; calm & pleasant channel.
> 12. [태웅쌤 - Comprehensible Input Korean](https://www.youtube.com/@ComprehensibleInputKorean)’s [[Lv.A0-1] Beginner TPRS Series](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLq3xYXNB0JzAlAc2g_Fy9KgpRnrIHKEOE), [unpacking](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLq3xYXNB0JzCgbavo6-l0YqxC-x2FvErI), [hidden folks](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLq3xYXNB0JzBcUvt4hobiQUiLAG9Gheel) & [stray](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLq3xYXNB0JzB8F1hPplMItaCh6wx5TDlT): 26+ hours, imo his most comprehensible video game stuff.
> 13. [한글용사 아이야](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLKDPDirFfxZQUw1oT3WfU1k31GwhyWFGW): 70+ hours; kids show, i love my hangul power rangers. ❤️💙💛
> 14. [Comprehensible Korean Language](https://www.youtube.com/@ComprehensibleKoreanLanguage)’s [beginner playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLp4wOE-uDDEvYt9CgVElFoSvOyfwywa5D): 25+ hours; mostly video game playthroughs.
> 15. [Muzzy in Gondoland](https://muzzyonline.com/catalog/search?category_id=15617): 4 hours; technically requires a subscription but offers a free trial, pretty famous for English learning & has a Korean version. 🛸
> 16. [Blippi Korean](https://www.youtube.com/@BlippiKorean): easy preschooler show, dubbed. 🚶‍♂️
> 17. [Numberblocks 넘버블럭스](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCR9zv3dquE6GURdKGJkVz-g): Obviously great for numbers (both native and Sino-Korean!), but the short dialogue is also really clear. A bit heavy on songs but overall super watchable, dubbed.
> 18. [Peppa Pig in Korean](https://www.youtube.com/@PeppaPigKoreanOfficial): 32 hours; preschooler show, dubbed. can be up to intermediate, depending on the season/episode. 🐷

> 21. [Bluey 블루이](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLPrhyopf_mxsqT9PTjTImW9Kp5lhT9nji): 20+ hours; preschooler show, dubbed. 💙
> 22. [Tayo 꼬마버스 타요](https://www.youtube.com/@%EA%BC%AC%EB%A7%88%EB%B2%84%EC%8A%A4%ED%83%80%EC%9A%94): preschooler show. 🚌
